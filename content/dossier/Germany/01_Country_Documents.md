# Germany Documents

These are the country-level items you should be ready for beyond the common checklist.

- Passport
- Degree / provisional degree and official transcript
- APS certificate for many Indian applicants (check each university process)
- English test score; Medium of Instruction is not always accepted
- Course descriptions / syllabus for math, algorithms, programming, statistics
- CV and statement materials when required
- Blocked account / visa funding proof later in the visa stage
