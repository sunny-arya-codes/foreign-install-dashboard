## Program shortlist
### 1. MSc Data Science
- Degree type: Masters
- My fit label: Medium
- Why this matters: Strong option if your transcript clearly shows enough math + CS credits.
Published requirement snapshot:
- Bachelor degree or equivalent with 180 ECTS / credits
- At least 20 LP in mathematics, including linear algebra/calculus and probability/statistics
- At least 10 LP in computer science, including algorithms and a higher programming language
- English C1
- Application deadline stated as May 31 each year for the winter intake
Typical documents to prepare:
- Self-disclosure form
- Transcript with relevant math/CS courses highlighted
- Course descriptions / syllabus
- Degree / provisional degree
- English certificate
- Timing note: A realistic 2027 target if your course mapping is prepared carefully.
