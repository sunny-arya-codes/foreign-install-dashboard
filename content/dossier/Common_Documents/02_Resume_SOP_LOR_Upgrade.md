# Resume, SOP, and LOR Upgrade Plan

## Resume
Your current resume is strong for jobs, but for admissions you should also prepare an academic-facing version.

Add or improve:
- A compact Education section near the top
- Coursework grouped around ML / math / CS foundations
- Research-style project descriptions with methods, datasets, metrics, and outcomes
- Links to GitHub, papers, demos, and technical blog posts

Reduce or compress:
- Excess operational detail that matters more for hiring than for graduate school
- Long bullet chains that do not show academic depth or research ability

## SOP
Your strongest narrative is:
- Data + software + GenAI in production
- IIT Madras quantitative training
- Clear move from builder to advanced applied AI / data systems / ML research practitioner

Each SOP should answer:
- Why this programme, not generic CS
- Why now
- What exact skills / labs / thesis topics you want
- Why you are a strong return on scholarship funding

## LORs
Ideal mix:
- 1 academic recommender from IIT Madras who can speak about rigor and coursework
- 1 academic or project mentor who can comment on ML / data / math readiness
- 1 manager or senior engineer from FreshMenu for production ownership and maturity

If possible, get at least 2 academic-style letters for PhD-heavy applications.
