# Common Documents Checklist

Last checked: 2026-04-08

These are the documents you should prepare once and then reuse across most countries.

## Identity and academic basics
- Passport (valid well beyond visa period)
- IIT Madras provisional degree / final degree certificate when available
- Official transcripts / semester-wise grade sheets
- CGPA to percentage / grading-scale explanation if available from IIT Madras
- Updated resume / CV in admissions format

## Core application documents
- Statement of Purpose (master’s version)
- Research statement / research interests note (PhD version)
- 2-3 Letters of Recommendation
- Work experience letters / relieving letter / role confirmation letters
- Portfolio of projects: GitHub, deployed demos, technical writeups
- Proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL/PTE/Duolingo depending country)

## Optional but high-value upgrades
- Academic resume version separate from job resume
- One polished research-oriented project writeup in PDF
- One preprint / workshop paper / technical report if you can produce one in 2026
- One clean transcript of relevant courses (ML, DL, DBMS, probability, linear algebra, DSA)

## Financial / visa-side documents
- Bank statement / sponsor statement
- Affidavit of support if family-sponsored
- Income proof / tax return if required by visa or scholarship
- Passport-size photographs in digital and print form
- Vaccination / medical documents where needed

## Important note
- As of 2026-04-08, many Fall 2026 deadlines are already closed. Build documents now for Spring/Fall 2027.
- Final eligibility is decided by the university. This folder marks realistic options based on published requirements and your current profile.
