# Tests and Exams

## Recommended default plan
- IELTS Academic target: overall 7.0+, with no band below 6.5
- TOEFL iBT target: 95-100+ if you prefer US-heavy applications
- Skip GRE unless a later US shortlist clearly rewards it

## Why this matters for your profile
- A stronger English score can partially offset a borderline CGPA.
- Scholarship committees like clean, unambiguous language proof.
- Germany/FU Berlin and Saarland have stricter English expectations on some programmes.

## Suggested test timeline
- April-May 2026: diagnostic test + prep plan
- June-July 2026: first IELTS/TOEFL attempt
- August 2026: retake only if needed

## Country notes
- Germany: some programmes accept only specific English proofs; MOI is not always accepted.
- Finland / UK / US / Australia / New Zealand: English requirements are standard but score thresholds vary.
- Taiwan / Singapore / South Korea: English may still be required depending on prior medium of instruction and programme policy.
