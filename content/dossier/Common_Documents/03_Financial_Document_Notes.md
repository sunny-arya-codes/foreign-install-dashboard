# Financial Document Notes

## Keep ready
- Savings proof / bank balance certificate
- Sponsor affidavit if parents or guardian are supporting you
- Income tax returns / salary slips if consulate asks for them
- Education loan sanction letter if relevant

## Country-specific examples
- Germany: blocked account / proof of funds later for visa stage
- Norway: proof of funding is checked early and tuition must also be covered
- US: I-20 financial proof after admission
- UK / Australia / New Zealand: visa-stage financial evidence rules vary by route

## Strategy
Because your budget is tight, do not design the plan around large self-funding. Build your list around:
- no/low tuition countries
- tuition-waiver scholarships
- funded PhD / MRes routes
- thesis-based programmes with assistantship potential
