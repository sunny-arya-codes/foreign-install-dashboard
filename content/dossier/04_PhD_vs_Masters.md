# PhD vs Masters

## Current recommendation
Apply to both, but do not split attention equally.

## Masters is currently more realistic because
- Your profile already proves technical execution and industry maturity.
- You have a 4-year bachelor’s degree from IIT Madras.
- Your CGPA is workable for many master’s programmes if the rest of the file is strong.

## Direct funded PhD is currently harder because
- Your resume does not yet show publications.
- Top PhD committees will look for clear research readiness, not just job strength.

## Best compromise
- Apply widely to scholarship-friendly master’s programmes.
- Also apply to a smaller number of funded research / PhD options in New Zealand, South Korea, Singapore, and selected US/Australia cases.
