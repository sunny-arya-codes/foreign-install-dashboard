## Program shortlist
### 1. MSc in Data Science and Machine Learning
- Degree type: Masters
- My fit label: Stretch
- Why this matters: Directly relevant to your profile; admissions are competitive and quantitatively oriented.
Published requirement snapshot:
- Bachelor’s Honours or 4-year bachelor’s degree in a quantitative science, engineering, or computing science
- TOEFL minimum 85 / IELTS minimum 6.0 if prior university instruction was not completely in English
- Selection is explicitly competitive
Typical documents to prepare:
- Degree / transcript
- English test when needed
- Programme application documents through NUS system
- Timing note: The page still displays recent application cycles; use it as a pattern and watch the next intake release.
