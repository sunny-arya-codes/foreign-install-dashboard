# Scholarship Strategy

## Highest-priority scholarship routes for you
- Germany: DAAD + low/no tuition public universities
- Taiwan: MOE Taiwan Scholarship + university scholarships
- South Korea: KAIST scholarship / GKS / university funding
- New Zealand: doctoral scholarships + domestic-fee PhD structure
- Singapore: SINGA / SUTD research fellowships / NUS-NTU funded research routes

## Medium-priority scholarship routes
- Finland: tuition-fee waivers at Helsinki / Aalto / Tampere
- UK: Chevening, GREAT, university-specific discounts
- Australia: RTP / faculty scholarships / university fee waivers

## Low-priority scholarship routes
- US: department assistantships, thesis-MS funding, funded PhD only
- Norway: very limited value for your budget case

## Hard truth
Do not design your plan around winning a single famous scholarship.
Design it around multiple lower-risk outcomes:
- low tuition country + modest scholarship
- funded research route
- country where living cost and tuition together remain survivable
