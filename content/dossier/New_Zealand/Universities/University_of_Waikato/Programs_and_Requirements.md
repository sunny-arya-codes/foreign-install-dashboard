## Program shortlist
### 1. Master of Data Science
- Degree type: Masters
- My fit label: High
- Why this matters: A very usable realistic target because the regulations allow exceptional-case admission based on academic merit and relevant experience.
Published requirement snapshot:
- Admission normally expects a bachelor degree with at least a B average in advanced papers or equivalent
- Regulations explicitly allow exceptional consideration based on academic merit and relevant experience
Typical documents to prepare:
- Degree / transcript
- English proof
- Standard application package
- Timing note: One of your better realistic coursework targets.
Official sources:
- [MDSc regulations](https://www.waikato.ac.nz/int/about/calendar/regulations/masters/mdsc/)
- [2026-2027 international guide](https://www.waikato.ac.nz/assets/Uploads/Study-with-us/Prospectus/2026-International-Student-Guide-University-of-Waikato.pdf)

### 2. PhD / doctoral path
- Degree type: PhD
- My fit label: Medium
- Why this matters: Good backup PhD destination; international full-time PhD students may be eligible for domestic NZ annual tuition fees.
Published requirement snapshot:
- Higher research degree entry depends on qualifying degree and research preparation
- University notes that new international PhD students studying full-time may be eligible for domestic tuition levels
Typical documents to prepare:
- Invitation / support from supervisor where needed
- Research proposal
- Referees
- Timing note: Use if NZ PhD route becomes primary.
Official sources:
- [PhD and doctoral entry](https://www.waikato.ac.nz/int/study/apply/phd-and-doctoral/)
