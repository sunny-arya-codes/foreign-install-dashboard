# University of Auckland

Last checked: 2026-04-08

## Fit summary
- Academic fit: High
- Funding fit: Strong for PhD / Medium for Masters
- Why shortlisted: Best NZ option for you overall because both Data Science master’s and low-fee PhD path exist.

## Program shortlist

- Degree type: Masters
- My fit label: High
- Why this matters: Good technical master’s option if you want a taught degree in NZ.

- Catalogue page states GPA-based entry expectations and programme structure

### 2. PhD in Computer Science / Data Science-aligned research
- Degree type: PhD
- My fit label: High
- Why this matters: One of the most financially attractive PhD routes in your whole list because international PhD students can pay domestic-level tuition if resident in NZ.
Published requirement snapshot:
- International PhD students pay the same annual tuition fee as domestic PhD students if resident in New Zealand during study
- University doctoral scholarships page shows stipend plus tuition-fee support
Typical documents to prepare:
- Research proposal / interest statement
- Potential supervisor fit
- Academic CV
- Strong letters
- Timing note: Excellent medium-term target if you build research signal in 2026.
Official sources:
- [Doctoral fees](https://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/study/fees-and-money-matters/tuition-fees/doctoral-fees.%E5%86%A0%E4%BA%9A%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85%E4%BD%93%E8%82%B2%EF%BC%9Ahtml.html)
