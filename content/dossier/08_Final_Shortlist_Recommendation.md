# Final Shortlist Recommendation

This is the most practical application direction based on the current profile, budget constraint, and current research.

## Primary Countries

### 1. Germany
- Best overall value
- Public-university structure keeps tuition pressure low
- Strong match for data science / AI / CS

Recommended starting targets:
- Saarland University
- Freie Universitat Berlin
- FAU Erlangen-Nurnberg

### 2. Taiwan
- Very strong technical upside for the cost
- Better scholarship environment than many Western destinations
- Good fit for AI, CS, and data-related programmes

Recommended starting targets:
- National Taiwan University
- National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University

### 3. South Korea
- Best if you want scholarship-backed AI / CS routes
- Good for MS-to-PhD or direct research routes

Recommended starting targets:
- KAIST
- UNIST
- POSTECH

### 4. Finland
- High-quality English-taught master’s ecosystem
- Only worth serious effort if you pursue tuition waivers aggressively

Recommended starting targets:
- University of Helsinki
- Tampere University
- Aalto University

### 5. New Zealand
- Particularly attractive for PhD
- Better economics for doctoral study than for coursework master’s

Recommended starting targets:
- University of Auckland
- University of Waikato
- Victoria University of Wellington

## Secondary Countries

### Singapore
- Use for high-upside funded or research-backed opportunities
- Do not make coursework-heavy options your main budget plan

### UK
- Use where scholarship or automatic fee discount materially reduces total cost
- Avoid broad self-funded applications

### Australia
- Better for RTP or research-linked funding than for plain coursework

## Low-Priority Countries

### US
- Apply only where funding path is real
- Prefer funded PhD or thesis-MS with assistantship chance

### Norway
- Keep as a low-priority backup only

## Best Application Mix

If you want a balanced and realistic plan, use this mix:

- `8-10` master’s applications across Germany, Taiwan, Finland, and New Zealand
- `4-6` funded research or PhD applications across South Korea, New Zealand, Singapore, and selected US/Australia cases
- `2-3` scholarship-sensitive UK/Australia/Singapore applications only if cost still makes sense

## Ideal Final Portfolio

The cleanest final portfolio for your profile would look like this:
