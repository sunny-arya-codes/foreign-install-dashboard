# UK Overview

Last checked: 2026-04-08

## Why this country is / is not a fit
- Fastest master’s route (usually 1 year), but financially dangerous without scholarship support.
- Use the UK only where scholarship probability or strong job ROI is high enough.
- Do not self-fund broadly across many UK universities with a tight budget.
