## Program shortlist
### 1. MSc Computer Science
- Degree type: Masters
- My fit label: Medium
- Why this matters: Only worth considering if the prerequisites map very cleanly and you are comfortable with the financial burden.
Published requirement snapshot:
- Bachelor in computer science / informatics or similar
- Approx. 50 ECTS in informatics/computer science, 25 ECTS mathematics, 5 ECTS statistics, 7.5 ECTS physics
- Minimum average comparable to Norwegian C
- Non-EU deadline shown as 15 Nov - 1 Dec on Study in Norway listing
Typical documents to prepare:
- Course descriptions are explicitly important
- Degree / transcript
- English proof where needed
- Funding documents
- Timing note: Backup only.
